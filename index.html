About the League

What is NCAL?

NCAL, or the North Carolina Academic League, is an organization dedicated to boosting the state, quality, and size of Quizbowl within North Carolina at the high school level. Currently based in Chapel Hill, NCAL aims to support teams from the Research Triangle and beyond by hosting regularly scheduled tournaments at a more affordable cost.

We are especially committed to supporting rural, Title 1, and underrepresented schools within North Carolina to play quizbowl and qualify for National level tournaments.

NCAL envisions a future where North Carolina contains both thriving local circuits, alongside a stronger, unified network stretching across the state.

NCAL Season Pass

Starting this year, we are offering an institutional NCAL Season Pass program to ease programmatic registration costs for new and growing teams across the state circuit.

Eligibility Breakdown

  • Newly Established Programs: Teams established starting from the 2026-2027 school year are eligible to register for any official NCAL tournament completely free.
  • Developing Programs with Need: Teams established starting from the 2025-2026 school year (at an institutional age of 2 years) with demonstrated financial or operational need are eligible to pay for a seasonal pass for a flat fee of $250 across all circuit events. This represents an estimated value of $540.

More information about the season pass can be found on the community forums.

Statistics Sharing Service

Want hands-on assistance uploading comprehensive full statistics reports for your next tournament event? We have your program covered from start to finish at zero operational cost.

Submission Instructions

Simply compile and transmit either of the following documentation variants to our administrative staff:

  • Clear legible images or scanned copies of physical paper scoresheets.
  • An accessible hyperlink routing to your program's active digital scoresheets.
Email files directly to: ncalquizbowl@gmail.com

The Result: Our staff will handle processing your recorded data to construct a fully standardized statistics file optimized and compatible with submission protocols for both the local forums and the official National Association for Quiz Bowl Tournaments (NAQT) website.
